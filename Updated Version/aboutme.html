About Me

My friends call me Trina..

I am from the East Seoik and New Ireland Provinces of Papua New Guinea.

I like Crocheting, Reading, watching Korean and Chinease Drama and Anime. Watching Korean and Chinease drama is fun to me because I like to listen to the language and pick up the some of the words to learn later.

I have been studying the korean alphabet ever since and can now make out the individual sounds from the korean letters in a sentence. Not the words though.
